Artifact 01

ECHO AI

I've started this project with the core motive to learn about AI integration, system security, and UX. And in about 30 days, it resulted THE ECHO AI. This app generates high-quality captions and headlines for products to list on e-commerce applications and social media.

Problem

Copy-writing each day of the week, for 5-10 products create too much friction. Even though, directly generation content with AI requires sending it product info.

Approach

Build a system where copy-writing is just a matter of 2 clicks.

Outcome

A software that has the capability to save >5 hours every week on copy-writing.
